Privacy Policy

Veritas — No Bias. No Agenda. Only Truth.
Last updated: April 2026

The Short Version

Veritas does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your fact-checks stay on your device. We have no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics that identify you.

What Veritas Does

Veritas is a fact-checking application that analyses claims, URLs, and images using live web sources. When you submit a claim, the app sends the claim text to third-party services (OpenAI, Serper, Tavily) for analysis and source retrieval. The verdict is returned to your device and displayed.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information. We do not create user accounts. We do not use cookies. We do not track your browsing activity.

Data That Stays on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers:

• Fact-check history (your recent verdicts)
• App preferences (dark mode, overlay settings)
• Rate limit counters (daily check counts)
• Cached search results (to reduce repeated API calls)

You can clear all local data at any time through Settings → Clear History, or by clearing the app's storage in Android Settings.

Third-Party Services

When you perform a fact-check, the claim text (not your identity) is sent to:

OpenAI — for claim analysis and verdict generation. OpenAI Privacy Policy

Serper — for live web search results. Serper Privacy Policy

Tavily — for supplementary search and article extraction. Tavily Privacy Policy

Google AdMob — if you voluntarily choose to watch a support video, Google AdMob may collect device-level advertising data as described in the Google Privacy Policy. Ads are never shown without your explicit action.

These services receive only the claim text or image content you submit. They do not receive your name, device ID, or any identifying information from Veritas.

Images

When you upload an image or capture a screenshot for fact-checking, the image is sent to OpenAI for text extraction and analysis. The image is processed in memory and deleted from your device's temporary storage immediately after analysis. We do not store, retain, or share your images.

Overlay and Accessibility Service

Veritas offers a floating overlay button that works on top of other apps. This requires Android's "Display over other apps" permission. The overlay does not monitor, record, or access content from other applications — it only activates when you tap it.

The optional screenshot feature uses Android's Accessibility Service solely to capture the current screen when you explicitly tap "Capture Screenshot." The screenshot is processed for fact-checking and immediately deleted. The accessibility service does not monitor your activity, keystrokes, or any other interactions.

Children's Privacy

Veritas is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
